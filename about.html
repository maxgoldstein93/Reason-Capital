About US

Reason is a North American focused private investment firm that manages a series of diversified partnerships that sponsor leveraged buyouts of middle market companies exhibiting viable, intriguing and compelling business opportunities. On a very selective basis, we serve as a lead investor in venture capital stage companies. We operate coast to coast, with our main offices strategically located in New York, New York and Santa Monica, California. Reason is a long term value oriented investor that partners with experienced management teams in acquiring, developing, or improving leading companiesâ€™ performances. Our internally developed performance models, based on portfolio company(s) profitability, have helped us to achieve superior returns. The end results enriches our capital base, provides superior returns to our partners and allows us to attract and retain a talented team of professionals. Our principals and the majority of our staff average more than twenty years in transactional and operating experience. Our goal is to continue to build a strong record of successful completed transactions and to generate substantial long-term value for our partners. Reason is a value-oriented, non industry selective investor, with significant relationships that span across many different business segments. We continually strive to discover strong, long-term investments that will provide superior risk-adjusted returns for our investors. Reason purposely focuses our attention towards assets that fit into one of two boxes:

  • Traditional Companies run by strong managers with superior or recognizable brand names, competitive positions protected by barriers to entry, robust free cash flow and sustainable future growth outlooks.
  • Non Traditional: Situations characterized as opportunistic, complex and/or convoluted transactions involving underperforming companies. In many cases they are turnaround companies that show promise; have underachieving balance sheets; past, present or future litigation issues or environmental problems or are categorized as special situations.

When we find a company that meets our standards, we are quick and aggressive in our efforts to secure the transaction. What distinguishes us from others is the extreme flexibility and creativity we exhibit in structuring those transactions and our follow through as a value-added partner. The record shows that the Reason our portfolio companies have patiently and steadily grown was based upon the right combination of perseverance, vision and capital.
