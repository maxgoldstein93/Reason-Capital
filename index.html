About US

Reason is a North American focused private investment firm that manages a series of diversified partnerships that sponsor leveraged buyouts of middle market companies exhibiting viable, intriguing and compelling business opportunities. On a very selective basis, we serve as a lead investor in venture capital stage companies. We operate coast to coast, with our main offices strategically located in New York, New York and Santa Monica, California. Reason is a long term value oriented investor that partners with experienced management teams in acquiring, developing, or improving leading companies’ performances. Our internally developed performance models, based on portfolio company(s) profitability, have helped us to achieve superior returns. The end results enriches our capital base, provides superior returns to our partners and allows us to attract and retain a talented team of professionals. Our principals and the majority of our staff average more than twenty years in transactional and operating experience. Our goal is to continue to build a strong record of successful completed transactions and to generate substantial long-term value for our partners. Reason is a value-oriented, non industry selective investor, with significant relationships that span across many different business segments. We continually strive to discover strong, long-term investments that will provide superior risk-adjusted returns for our investors. Reason purposely focuses our attention towards assets that fit into one of two boxes:

  • Traditional Companies run by strong managers with superior or recognizable brand names, competitive positions protected by barriers to entry, robust free cash flow and sustainable future growth outlooks.
  • Non Traditional: Situations characterized as opportunistic, complex and/or convoluted transactions involving underperforming companies. In many cases they are turnaround companies that show promise; have underachieving balance sheets; past, present or future litigation issues or environmental problems or are categorized as special situations.

When we find a company that meets our standards, we are quick and aggressive in our efforts to secure the transaction. What distinguishes us from others is the extreme flexibility and creativity we exhibit in structuring those transactions and our follow through as a value-added partner. The record shows that the Reason our portfolio companies have patiently and steadily grown was based upon the right combination of perseverance, vision and capital.

Our Team

Jeffrey A. Gugick has over 20 years experience as a banker, investor and manager specializing in structured and corporate finance, private equity and commercial real estate. Mr. Gugick is a Managing Director of Reason Capital Group LLC, a private equity investment firm focused on middle market buyouts, turnarounds and opportunistic investment in real estate. Mr. Gugick previously was Managing Director and Chief Financial Officer for the Utendahl Group ("Utendahl"), which included Utendahl Capital Partners, L.P. (a boutique investment bank, broker/dealer), Utendahl Capital Management, L.P. (a $2.4 billion fixed income asset manager) and private equity funds. In his tenure at Utendahl from 1995 to 2003, Mr. Gugick served as both a senior investment banker and an executive at the firm, providing strategic direction, management and business development. Jeff also created and managed the private equity fund operations at Utendahl. This included, creating and raising of over $300 million of institutional capital for three first-time private investment funds in commercial real estate, private equity and mezzanine debt. He has also managed multiple commercial and residential mortgage asset sales aggregating over $2.5 billion resulting in significant recoveries for clients including the FHA, FDIC and Financial Institutions. Prior to Utendahl, Mr. Gugick held commercial real estate workout/restructuring positions at Greenthal Harlan Realty Services Company, (an affiliate of Castle Harlan, Inc.), the Dime Savings Bank (now J.P. Morgan Chase) and Citibank N.A. During this time he developed managerial skills in lending, problem asset resolution and corporate finance. Mr. Gugick has his B.S. in Business Administration with majors in Finance and Marketing Management from Syracuse University, his MBA in Finance from Emory University's Goizueta Graduate School of Business Administration, and has also completed the Citicorp Institute for Global Finance executive credit training program. Mr. Gugick is a board member of the Boy Scouts of America's Greater NY Council/Manhattan Borough Board, a member of The Economic Club of New York and a member of Financial Executives International (FEI).
Marc Schuster has 20 years of experience in originating corporate and real estate debt and buying and trading distressed loans. The firm researches and markets the financing and sale of real estate investments to public and private investors, including oversight of all aspects of the negotiations, contracts, and closings of multi-million dollar commercial and residential real estate transactions.
